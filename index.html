Thal University Bhakkar Punjab Pakistan

Thal University Bhakkar (TUB) is a public-sector university located in Bhakkar, Punjab, Pakistan. It was established in 2022 under the Act of the Provincial Assembly and is the first public-sector university in the Thal region of Punjab.
